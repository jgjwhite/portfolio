Background

Hi, thanks for taking the time to look through my portfolio. I’m a designer who recently graduated from the York Sheridan design program in Toronto, and I’m focused on the digital space, namely web and mobile application design. The challenge of creating products that fulfill a need fascinates me, and I hope to continue that work in the future. I’m particularly interested in design's role in easing the adoption of new technology. In my spare time, I tinker with motion design and web development.

Feel free to reach out to me for work or collaboration.
