Web Design

Ursa LED

Background

Ursa is a state of the art LED manufacturer based in California. Ursa’s previous website lacked promotional appeal, was not responsive, and did not do a good job showcasing their product line. Mighty had created a brand refresh, and were applying the new look to Ursa’s website when I began.

Responsive Web Design

I began with a subset of the required page designs, desktop only. I was responsible for designing the remaining pages, and applying responsive patterns to create deisgns for mobile and tablet devices.

Structuring Ursa's Product Line

Each lighting product belongs to a certain line and application(s). Product lines can include Helios, Optilux, and Horticulture, while applications refers to products that perform well in stadiums, or greenhouses, etc. Ursa wanted visitors to be able to filter on both product line and application in order to see which lights best suited their needs. I organized Ursa’s products into a spreadsheet matrix, and used this to ideate different navigation designs.

Developer Collaboration

Our team used Zeplin to collaborate with Reflektor. The tool was helpful for importing assets and commenting on some of the more nuanced details of the mock-ups, but it didn’t replace regularly communicating directly with Reflektor’s developers.
