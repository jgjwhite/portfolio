Tressle Home Decor

NYC Tech Day 2017

Tressle is a home furnishing & décor search engine that allows visitors to search across home retailers and find the best value on products. The founders had won a spot as exhibitors at NYC Tech Day 2017, and wanted a product demo video and landing page.

Product Demo Video

The video was to be shown on a TV display in their Tech Day booth, and the goal when I was creating this video was to communicate the value proposition of Tressle's product: the ability to search across home retailers like wayfair, houzz, target, etc., compare prices on similar products, and one-click buy at checkout.

Landing Page

A landing page was designed to show off Tressle’s features in more detail and collect e-mails for their product launch.

Share Page

Tressle set up a contest to gamify their link sharing. The goal of this page was to showcase a visitors points and progress, to incentivize them to continue sharing until they unlocked all possible prize entries.
