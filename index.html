Bio

I'm an interactive designer seeking to work at the intersection of technology, data and design. I enjoy the challenge of creating products that people will use. Beginning with researching the problem and then exploring solutions through an iterative process, I believe that what makes a solution good is that it has a measurable effect on the end user. I’m particularly interested in design's role in easing the adoption of technology. In my spare time, I tinker with illustration and coding. I’ve found that exploring different design mediums has helped to strengthen my creative process.

Thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio, and feel free to reach out to me for collaboration or work opportunities.
