May 2016

Big Bloom Cost Rationalization Tool

The Bloomberg Per Security Data License enables financial professionals to request customizable data fields on a user-specified set of securities. This flexibility is highly valuable, and therefore widely used; however keeping track of the pricing of these requests firm-wide can be confusing, and often leads to cost spirals. The Big Bloom Cost Rationalization tool plays the role of granting users of the Per Security Data License greater insight into their costs and usage detail, and has the capability of highlighting large opportunities for savings.

I worked with the team at Market Data Company to build the interface for their Beta release of the Big Bloom tool, which would later be marketed to buy-side financial firms. Applying User Experience deliverables and UI patterns, I developed a set of mockups for feedback and iteration, which ultimately informed the final interface design.

Collaborators: Serge Kaleis (Development), Maurice Severin, Rob Benedetto (Business owners).

Tressle Access Landing Page

Tressle.com is an online marketplace specializing in home design, selling both major interior design brands as well as independent and boutique designer goods at wholesale prices. The Tressle Access initiative invited interior designers to purchase an exclusive annual membership in exchange for premium pricing and unlimited access. I designed and built the landing page that was later circulated to more than 5000 recipients as part of an e-mail marketing campaign.

Collaborators: Juliana White, Fatima Yusuf (Business Owners).
